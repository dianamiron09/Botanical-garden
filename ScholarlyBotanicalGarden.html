Abstract

The “Brera Botanical Garden” website is a way for people to visualize what the Botanical Garden and to communicate with the garden’s specialists, my signing up, giving feedback or even buy different items.
This project presents details about the Brera Botanical Garden and ways to interact with its specialists.

Introduction

Nowadays, everything needs a website. Otherwise, you risk looking like a tiny operation that isn’t going to be able to deliver great service. Having a professional looking website is a very simple way of boosting your reputation. Potential customers are increasingly tech-savvy, and before trying out a small business, the first place most people turn is the Internet. If you don’t have your own website, but your competitors do, potential customers will start to wonder why.

Website Pages

Home

The homepage presents the overall information about the botanical garden situated and Milan, displays pictures and also provides an easy way to contact the representatives of the botanical garden, through a small form.


Through the form, the user can send comments or requests which will be automatically sent to the feedback email.

At the bottom of the page there are links to the botanical garden’s Social Media pages.

About

The About page contains more information about the Botanical Garden found in Milan

Our Gardens

The Our Gardens page contains details about the gardens and the plants found in each of them, as well as when is the best time to visit a certain garden.

Visit Us

The Visit Us page presents information about the Botanical Garden's open hours, services, tips and rules.

Guided Tours

The Guided Tours page presents the options and schedules people have to visit the gardens with a guide, in different languages.

Exhibitions

The Exhibitions page contains details about future exhibitions which will be held in the Brera gardens. For each of them a date interval and open hours are provided.

Start gardening with our help!

The website provides the user the posibility to register, in order to receive emails from specialists. The emails can contain details about plant species, tips and tricks to start gardening.

In order to register, the user has to insert his/her name and e-mail address. He also has to select the level of gardening, check whether he wants to receive emails from different gardening companies and also add comments in case he has certain preferences.

After the registration, the user immediately becomes a customer and is added to the database. He also receives a welcoming email.

Garden Shops

The website offers the customers the posibility to buy merch or different items which are sold in order to help different campaigns.

Contact

Contact Form

The contact form is provided for people who just have a curiosity or want to know information about schedule changes, without having to become a customer and receive regular emails.

Plant Enquiry Form

The plant enquiry form is made for plant lovers who either want to donate a rare species of plant or in case they want to own a certain species of plant.

Professional Study Visit/Exchange Program

This page is for organised visits or requests for exhibitions or events.
