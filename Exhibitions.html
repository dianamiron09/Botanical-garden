Beneath Tide, Running Forest Exhibition

Date 24 Nov 2018 to 14 Apr 2019
Time 9am to 6pm
Venue CDL Green Gallery (beside Level 2 of the Botany Centre)

Beneath Tide, Running Forest

An art and science exploration of marine biodiversity

Curated by Dr. Wang Ruobing

Globally, marine biodiversity is under threat – pollution is on the rise and oceanic life is disappearing. Yet, marine life thrives. This exhibition is a conscious response to the beauty and ruin of these majestic ecosystems from four local artists. It is also a rallying cry that something needs to be done by everyone before it is too late. Learn more about these fragile yet resilient ecosystems, and the efforts invested into restoring them.

A group exhibition by our local artists.

This exhibition is held in conjunction with the International Year of the Reefs 2018. To learn more about the programmes and get involved, visit us at https://www.nparks.gov.sg/iyor.


ITALY – Botanical Art Worldwide Exhibition 2019
A Flourishing Heritage – Portraits of Italian Flora

Date May 18 – June 18
Time 9am to 6pm

Italy too has joined Botanical Art Worldwide – Linking people to plants through botanical art; a project initiated by the American Society of Botanical Artists. The exhibition in Italy will be held at the Brera Botanic Garden, established in 1860 and declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO in 1997. Recently, a new modern sector called The Biodiversity Garden was inaugurated adjacent to the ancient Garden of Simples which dates back to the 16th century. The exhibition has been organised by Floraviva, the Italian Association of Botanical Artists in collaboration with the Brera Botanic Garden.
